Great Backyard Bird Count
Thanks to Jenny Holbrook for arranging this culminating experience to Project Feeder Watch. We will take a short quarter mile walk down San Marco to  to Mission Nombre de Dios to see how many species of birds we can spot. We will send our tally to GBBC. This four day count, 2/15-18 this year, typically receives sightings from tens of thousands of people reporting over 600 species of birds in the US and Canada alone. This year anyone in the world can participate, and it will be exciting to see how many of the world's 10,240 bird species will be reported. Our kids have learned to recognize many birds and look forward to seeing how many we can spot. Students are welcome to bring binoculars but must be prepared to carry them. A permission slip for this trip went home yesterday. Please make sure your child is wearing appropriate walking shoes.
