Eagle Class News

The school year is almost over and our regular work days are winding down. Children are revisiting their favorite math materials and finishing up projects. Our big work now is preparation for the South America festival. Since we have been studying Amazonia, our class will create a rainforest in our classroom. We would like to serve foods from the rainforest for our snack. Please let me know if you can provide any of the following: pineapple, mangos, papaya, banana, cashews, Brazil nuts, oranges, guava, passion fruit, peanuts, coconuts, figs. Food can be brought to school on Monday morning, so we can prepare it. Thank you.

 

Students are encouraged to read over summer break. Use your library cards! Practice those math facts to keep your skills sharp. If you take a trip, keep a travel journal and write postcards to me, your friends, and relatives.

Fundraising Corner

A sincere and huge THANK YOU to everyone for all of the help and participation with our fundraisers this year!  Because of YOU we raised close to $34,000 this school year!!!  What great community support and teamwork!  CONGRATULATIONS & THANK YOU!!!

 

Of the money raised this year, we are thrilled that $1100 in donations was received at the Gala specifically for playground equipment.  We have a small team currently reaching out to various manufacturers of 7'H commercial grade metal Dome Climbers (like the one at the Lighthouse park) to get details, specifications, and quotes.  The dome climber was selected because it can fit within the confines of the current location, it can be moved to a new school location when the time comes, it allows kids to get their energy out in a positive way while working their muscles, it can accommodate the most amount of kids per sq. foot compared to other playground equipment, and, at around $3000, it provides the most bang for our buck given our limited funds.  

 

When the research and quote process is completed, we will bring our findings to the board to request approval to proceed with raising the remaining funds needed to purchase the equipment.  Stay tuned over the summer as we will keep you updated on the progress with the playground.

 

Finally, school is almost out, but Target Red Card and I-Give are always in!  Don't forget to recruit grandparents and other friends and relatives to signing up for these great, and easy passive fundraisers and designating SAPMS as the recipient school!
