BUILDING THE MATHEMATICAL MIND

Join us next Tuesday, October 29 at 6:00 for our first parent education event of the year. We will meet at the school, so we have access to the math materials. The Montessori math materials are some of the most beautiful and recognizable in our classrooms. Each new concept is introduced with a concrete material. Lessons with these lead your child from the concrete representation to pure abstraction. Learning is active, experiential and discovery based.

UPPER ELEMENTARY NEWS

Students enjoyed a Greek festival, the culminating activity for our study of ancient Greece, last Friday. Everyone dressed in a chiton accessorized by snake bracelets, beaded earrings, and shields. We enjoyed meze, a variety of small tasty dishes traditionally served as a backdrop for social gatherings. This was served with grape juice and honey cake for a dessert. We played some games played by children in ancient Greece like knucklebones and blind man's bluff. A series of foot races ended the day. Thanks to our parents for helping make the chitons and providing refreshments. We also finished the epic Black Ships Before Troy. Although we are moving on to ancient Rome, everyone wants to continue the tale by hearing The Odyssey.
