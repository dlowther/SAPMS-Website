UPPER ELEMENTARY NEWS:

Students are embarking on their first big research project. Everyone is researching a modern European country. After lessons on paraphrasing and selecting important facts, children have begun taking notes. They are using 3x5 cards, color coded for each main topic on their outline. For those who have not made it to the library for books, I have brought a selection to the classroom. This long term project will not be completed until after the Thanksgiving break. Please return your child's field trip permission slip and $6 by tomorrow, if you have not already done so.  Please note that the Fountain of Youth does charge for school programs.

LOWER ELEMENTARY NEWS:  
In the Lower Elementary Classes, students have been learning about France. They have
been making Eiffel Towers and had a full sensory experience of the sights, sounds and smells of France.
 In math, students are working on line studies, fractions and bead frame multiplication.
We are preparing for our Field trip to the Old Florida Museum and learning about the Native
Americans in preparation for Thanksgiving. If anyone would like to help fill our classroom
European basket with teachable items about Italy, it would be greatly appreciated. We are
looking for books; brochures and items children can use to help them experience this beautiful country. Please bring any items to your child's teacher upon returning from Thanksgiving break.
We look forward to our upcoming field trip on Thursday and Thanksgiving feast next week.
