HAPPY NEW YEAR!

I hope everyone enjoyed a restful and joyous winter break.   It was fun to see all the children yesterday.  They were excited to be back with their school friends again.  We are looking forward to this time of year when the children apply themselves to their work with renewed vigor.  Expect a productive new year!  SAPMS staff and I would like to thank everyone for the generosity extended to us at the year's end. It was most appreciated.
PROGRESS REPORTS

Your child's first written progress report will come home on Tuesday, January 14.  Reports for both Lower and Upper Elementary include concepts covering the entire three years of the program.  So there is no need for concern if there are items that are not marked.  Over the three year cycle of the program your child will touch on all the concepts listed.  Please contact your child's teacher with any questions or concerns about these reports.
COMMUNITY MEETING

Our second school Community Meeting will be on Tuesday, January 21 at the main library at 6:00.  These 'state of the school" meetings keep you up to date on what is happening at SAPMS.  You will hear from various committees and the board.  We will also have a special Montessori parent education activity, the Jenga Paradigm.  Mark your calendars and save the date.  Just a friendly reminder......your Contract of Commitment requires you to attend at least two of the three yearly Community Meetings.  If you did not attend the first one, your attendance is required at this one.
EUROPE FESTIVAL 

We are bringing our Europe studies to a close this month with a Europe Festival on Friday, January 17 at 1:45.  Children will display their research projects on their European country of choice.  We are asking for your help in preparing for this.  In addition to the work done at school, we would like each child to create a display that includes a traditional food, pictures, and artifacts if possible.  If your child has some article of clothing traditional in the country, please include that.  Each class will send home more specific instructions.  We encourage creativity in the presentations.

FREE WEBINAR  

Everyday many items come across my desk.  Most are discarded, but I thought this one would be of interest.

 

Florida's Charter Support Unit (CSU) is pleased to announce a new, free webinar that is scheduled for Thursday January 9, 2014 at 10:00 am.  

 

The title of this webinar is:

 

Parents and Charter Schools: Achieving Excellence through Innovation.  

 

You can register for this free presentation by visiting: https://www4.gotomeeting.com/register/544065935

 

This webinar will feature Henry Rose, Director of Parents for Charter Schools, a free organization that is dedicated to serving parents of charter school students. The presentation will primarily focus on how to best utilize your parents and encourage their participation in your charter school. This webinar should be beneficial to parent volunteers, administrators, teachers, board members and stakeholders interested in how to facilitate and improve parental involvement programs.

 

Unfortunately the time precludes my participation, but I would be interested to hear about if from anyone who is able to listen in. Please keep in mind that the needs and ways of participating in a Montessori school and often differ from more traditional programs.
