PROGRESS REPORTS

Your child's first written evaluation went home yesterday. Please contact your child's teacher if you did not receive yours or if you have questions. For those of you new to Montessori, this report probably looks very different than what you have been used to seeing.   Students are evaluated individually, and we look at the whole child. Social development and practical life skills (work habits) are assessed along with mastery of fundamental skills. Another form of assessment used is the students' portfolio of their work. We will have another parent visiting afternoon later in the month, where you will have the opportunity to review your child's portfolio.

COMMUNITY MEETING

Our next Community Meeting is next Tuesday, January 21 at 6:00 in the main library. We will provide child care at the school if there is sufficient interest. The cost for child care will be $5/child.
