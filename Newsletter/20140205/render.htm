LOWER ELEMENTARY NEWS

The children have been very excited as we have begun our studies of Asia. The past two weeks we have been focusing on China and the Chinese New Year. We have also begun studying the fundamental needs of humans, focusing on the material needs. In math, the children are working on memorization of facts, word problems, measurement, fractions, and operations using the checkerboard. For language, we have been learning about context clues, sentence analysis, and have begun incorporating the SRA reading lab into our daily reading practice. The children were very excited to hypothesize in their science notebooks when Ms. Lorraine conducted an experiment with both classes on static electricity. They are also learning about the layers of the Earth for science and we are preparing to study the timeline of life.
