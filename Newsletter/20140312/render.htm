SPRING BREAK
There is no school for students on Friday to give the teachers a work day. Thursday is a full day of school. Spring break begins Friday and continues through the following week. We will use this time to relax and recharge our batteries and hope that our students do the same. Everyone returns to school on Monday, March 24. 
NEW STUDIOS  

We began a new round of studios today with a new format. Each studio will meet two times instead of four. We are excited to have two parent run studios this session. Curtis Franklin is bringing Magic Time to SAPMS. He is demonstrating and teaching magic tricks. Dana Palermo and Angela Thompson are offering Herbology 101. Students sampled homemade root beer and tried out nature's toothbrush. Other offerings this session include mosaic stepping stones, yoga, crochet/ knitting, and track and field. The children enjoy the chance to try out new things, and it is a time when all three classes are mixed together. We will continue with this two session cycle for the rest of the year. We welcome more parent volunteers doing studios. This allows each group to be smaller, and you only need to have an activity for two sessions. Time spent preparing for and conducting studios count toward your service hour requirement.  
PARENT / TEACHER / STUDENT CONFERENCES

Conferences will be held after the break on Wednesday, March 26 and Thursday, March 27. Each class will have a sign-up sheep available at drop off and pick up on Monday, 3/24 and Tuesday, 3/25. On both Wednesday and Thursday dismissal will be at 11:45 to accommodate conferences. Students will not eat lunch at school. If there is interest, we will offer childcare on both of those days for $6/hour. Please let me know if you need this service.
FCAT PROCTOR / VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITY

We will need three proctors a day April 22 - April 25. This involves signing a security agreement and sitting quietly in the room while the students take this test. Students third grade and up are required to take this test. You may not proctor in a classroom with your own child. Send me an email if you can help with this.
