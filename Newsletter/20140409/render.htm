VOLUNTEER UPDATE 

We are happy to see more families taking advantage of the volunteer opportunities being offered, and thank you to those who have met and exceeded their hours. We are striving to build a stronger sense of community by providing opportunities to work with others (staff & families) to reach a common goal. Attending Community Meetings and Parent Education events provides opportunities to interact with other school families and become more informed about the Montessori method of education you have chosen for your child. Following are some ways to fulfill your 20 service hour commitment.

  • Visit the Volunteer Spot online to see what's available
  • Ask your child's teacher about any material making needs
  • Help with the Art Show
  • Help with the spring cleaning morning on May 3 - DOUBLE SERVICE HOURS!
  • Classroom guest speaker - share a cultural experience or an area of expertise
  • Help with an Earth Day recycled craft on April 25
  • Attend the last Parent Ed night on May 8
  • Still need a few FCAT proctors - contact Judi directly

ARE YOU SMARTER THAN A FIFTH GRADER ?
Our last parent education night will be held on Thursday, May 8 at 6:00. This will be a completely hands-on evening. We will set up Montessori materials from each level of the curriculum. You will be able to sit down and experience the apparatus the way your children do. Each lesson will be accompanied with a set of instructions and teachers will be on hand to guide you. This night will be a lot of fun and perhaps a bit humbling.
UPPER ELEMENTARY NEWS
Permission slips were sent home yesterday for our trip to the drinking water treatment plant. This trip will enhance our study of water and the water cycle. If you have not already done so, please provide a copy of A Land Remembered by Patrick Smith. Remember we are using the Student Edition Volume 1. This novel study will help bring our Florida history studies to life. Representatives of the Fish & Wildlife Conservation Commission will visit our class later this month to present a program on the Atlantic Right Whale.
