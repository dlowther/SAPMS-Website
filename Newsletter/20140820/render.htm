WELCOME TO THE NEW SCHOOL YEAR

On behalf of the staff of SAPMS, I am happy to welcome you to the 2014-15 school year. We look forward to a productive partnership with you to ensure our children grow to their highest potential. The Montessori Method focuses on allowing children to learn in a hands-on experiential setting.   Children are curious by nature and love to explore. Our learning environments are created to keep that joy of discovery alive.  Our Montessori materials promote brain development and enhance critical thinking skills.  Children learn through interaction with the materials and through collaboration with others.  Through the course of the school year, we invite you to come share your talents and your cultural backgrounds with our children that help broaden their world view. 

 

I will be teaching in the Upper Elementary classroom until 12:15 each day.  In the afternoons, I am available to take phone calls or meet with parents as needed.  You can also reach me by email at judi@staugustinemontessori.com.  I look forward to getting to know all our new parents and strengthening the school/home partnership with everyone. 

NEW ARRIVAL AND DISMISSAL TIMES & PROCEDURES 

Last month parents received an electronic copy of the revised Community Handbook and asked to review these new procedures.  We also handed out hard copies at our Meet the Teacher morning.  In case you missed both of these, here they are again.

 

Arrival:  Lower Elementary - 8:05 - 8:15       Upper Elementary - 8:15 - 8:25

 

Dismissal: LE - 2:45-3:00  Wednesdays:  1:45-2:00   UE - 3:00-3:15  Wednesdays: 2:00-2:15

 

These times are staggered to accommodate increased enrollment this year. Siblings of UE students and those carpooling with UE students will use UE arrival and dismissal times.  For the first few weeks of school, please place the paper with your child's name on your car dashboard.  Thank you for your attention to this.  We hope to make these transition times more streamlined and less stressful for everyone. Due to the space taken up by the modular ramp, you will not be able to park in front of it during drop off and pick up.

CLASSROOM / COMMUNITY MEETING 6:00-7:30 

As noted on your school calendar, our first all - school meeting will be held on Tuesday, August 26 at 6:00.  We will be adding something new this year.  From 6:00 - 6:30, parents will meet with their child's teacher in the classroom.  Teachers will explain the daily schedule, expectations, and answer your questions.  At 6:30, everyone will gather in our new modular classroom for the Community Meeting.  At this meeting you will be introduced to our staff and board and hear about our fundraising goals for the year. You will also receive a hard copy of the Community Handbook at the meeting. This is a great way to meet other parents and keep in touch with what is going on at the school.  And best of all, you'll get to see our new beautiful modular classroom.  You may count time at the meeting toward fulfillment of your 20 service hour requirement.
