PARENT / STUDENT / TEACHER CONFERENCES

It is time for our first set of conferences.  Please note that students attend the conference with their parents.  Our Montessori classrooms are child centered, so our conferences are too.  All students are active participants in their conferences, while older students actually lead the conference.  Student led conferences empower students to take responsibility for their own learning and be actively engaged in the learning process.  Students practice the real life skills of speaking with adults, advocating for themselves, and setting goals.


If you have not yet signed up for a Parent/Student/Teacher conference, please contact your child's teacher.  Conferences are set in half hour intervals.  Please arrive for and exit your conference in a timely manner.  If you arrive late, please understand that your conference will be cut short, so the conference following yours can begin on time.
