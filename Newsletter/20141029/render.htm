PARENT / STUDENT / TEACHER CONFERENCES

Students are looking forward to showing their work and portfolios to parents at the conference.  Please arrive on time and be ready to leave after 30 minutes, so the next family does not have to wait. If you would like a few minutes to speak to the teacher alone, please let the teacher know at the beginning of the conference.
ANNUAL FUND

If you have not yet received your annual fund letter, you will receive it at your conference.  It is our hope that everyone will donate to this campaign.  We have done our best to make sure each classroom is beautifully furnished and equipped.  The funding we get from the state is not sufficient to cover this and all of our other expenses.  We hope that everyone finds a way to give to this campaign.  Even small donations are appreciated.  We would like to be able to say that 100% of our families, staff, and board have donated.  Please give according to your ability. Thank you to those who have already contributed! 
SYKES & COOPER FARM CORN MAZE

Permission slips for this trip went home on Friday.   We will need volunteers to drive and chaperone.  If you have returned a form offering to drive, you may assume that we need you.  Teachers will only contact you if you are NOT needed to drive.  Remember all chaperones must have a completed background check in our office.  The cost is $5/student and chaperone.

Students should wear their SAPMS t-shirts and sneakers.  Bring your lunch and water bottle.  Because this day was included on the semester payment for pizza, we will do a make- up day in December.

 
MUSIC

Students have been enjoying their lively music classes with Ms. Beard. In addition to singing, they have an opportunity to try out some different percussion instruments.  UE students are playing recorders.  Everyone enjoyed doing the Skeleton Dance!
MONTESSORI CONFERENCE

School will be closed next Friday, November 7, so teachers can attend the Montessori Foundation Conference in Sarasota.  This is an opportunity for us to deepen our expertise as teachers, renew ourselves, and receive inspiration.  We also look forward to connecting with old friends and making new ones.

 

School is also closed the Monday following the conference, November 10, in honor of Veteran's Day.

Equal Exchange Fundraiser

This week kicks off our annual holiday Equal Exchange fundraiser.  If you love great coffee and superior quality chocolate, both organic and fairly traded, you're going to LOVE Equal Exchange!  By enjoying Equal Exchange, you join a movement to support small farmers and artisans from around the world.  Their products make great holiday gifts for family, friends and clients.  Your catalog went home in your child's folder on Tuesday.  Friends and family not in town can shop the online store at  http://fundraiser.equalexchange.coop/?fundraiser=SAPM001W .  Orders will be due on November 11th and checks for your total order can be made payable to SAPMS.  If you have any questions, contact Teri Aboulafia at taboulafia@hotmail.com.
