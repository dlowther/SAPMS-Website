UPPER ELEMENTARY NEWS

Our fifth and sixth year students have begun exploring the third Great Lesson, the coming of humans. Currently, we are looking at the various types of Australopithecus, the first hominids whose fossil remains were discovered in Africa. Math topics include equivalent figures, multiples, prime factorization, fractions, and statistics. Poetry and short stories are being covered in literature circles, and other language work includes paragraph sequencing, independent and subordinate clauses, and capitalization rules. Africa work includes the study of the desert and wetland biomes. Children can see that deserts cover two fifths of the African continent, while wetlands are found in only three widely separated places.  The UE class will enjoy a field trip to The Old Florida Museum on Monday, November 24.
