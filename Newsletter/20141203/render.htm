ANNUAL FUND / FUNDRAISING

We have gotten off to a good start with our Annual Fund but still have a long way to go.  Board member and parent, Endo Pascasio, created a display to track our progress. It is in the front window facing the car line near the gate.  In best Montessori fashion, he used the image of a golden bead ten bar to track our progress.  If you have not yet contributed to the Annual Fund, please consider doing so now.  You can make your donation now or make a pledge to donate after the first of the year.  Remember, we are a 501c3 corporation, so all donations are tax deductible.

We are hoping to get a good turnout for our fundraising meeting this evening.  We will meet at 6:00 in the modular classroom.  There's lots of work to be done to prepare for upcoming events. Your time at the meeting will count toward your service hours. 

 
WINTER FESTIVAL

As noted on our school calendar, our Winter Festival will be held on Thursday, December 18 at 6:00.  The children will do a short performance, then we will enjoy a potluck dinner.  Bring chairs and a dish to share.  We also ask that everyone bring their own plates, cutlery, and cups, so we can avoid using paper products.  Festivities will take place on the Lower Elementary play area. We would appreciate having a couple of volunteers to help with the clean-up.  This will count towards your service hours.

 

LOWER ELEMENTARY NEWS

The LE students enjoyed their field trip to the Fountain of Youth. They learned about early navigation in the planetarium, explored Timucua dwellings, and excavated shells. After the field trip, the students enjoyed making pine cone turkeys, creating leaves of thankfulness, and prepping their contributions for our Thanksgiving feast.
