HAPPY HOLIDAYS

What a busy time of year this is!  Our schedules are filled with errands, shopping, homes to decorate, packages to wrap, friends to see, cookies to bake.......  In the midst of all this activity, try to find an easier way to navigate this holiday season.  Make your family traditions the cornerstone of the season. These will become your child's most lasting memories. Choose which activities are most important to you and your family.  It's ok to miss an event or two.  Focus on the meaning of the holidays, whether your family is religious or not. What do the holidays mean to you?  Make that your focus, then share those values and meaning with your children. Shop with care.  As your children grow up they will remember the things you did together more than what gifts they received.


 However you celebrate this festive season, enjoy your beautiful families and have an awesome Winter Solstice, a Happy Hanukah, a Merry Christmas, and a Happy New Year!  I look forward to beginning the new year together.

 
WINTER FESTIVAL

We are all looking forward to gathering this Thursday evening at 6:00 for our Winter Festival. Following St. Augustine holiday tradition, you will be surrounded by twinkling white lights. Many thanks to Nicole and Lenny Conticello for installing them.

 

Upon arrival at 6:00, all students will gather in Ms. Heather's room to prepare. The children's performance will begin at 6:15. Please be prompt, as it is very disappointing for children to arrive late after the performance has begun. We will gather outside, so bring a chair and dress for the weather. After the music we will have a pot luck dinner. Please bring a dish or drinks (juice or water) to share. Also remember to bring your own plates, cutlery, and cups so we generate as little trash as possible. At the end of the evening, please remember to take your serving dishes and bowls home. We are still in need of a couple of volunteers to help with the clean up after dinner. This is an easy way to work toward your required 20 service hours.

 

The next day, Friday, December 19, is our last day of school before the break. All students will be dismissed early at 11:45/noon. There will be no lunch or aftercare. Teachers return on January 5 for a work day, and students return Tuesday, January 6.
