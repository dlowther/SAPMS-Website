HAPPY NEW YEAR!

The holidays are over and life is returning to normal.  At this time of year it is traditional to make resolutions for the coming year.  Following are some suggested resolutions for SAPMS families

  • Arrive at school on time.  All students should be in their classrooms by 8:30.  Tardy arrivals are disruptive to the students and teachers who are beginning their school day with a morning lesson or meeting.  Students who are late miss out on the morning greeting, important lessons and information.
  • Prioritize a good night's sleep.  Schedules are disrupted during breaks from school.  Make sure your child gets to bed early enough to be rested for upcoming school day.
  • Practice healthy eating habits.  Good nutrition promotes optimal brain functioning.  Help your child be ready to learn by providing a balanced diet of nutrient dense foods.
  • Resolve to complete your volunteer hours before the end of the year.  Our online Volunteer Spot is being updated with lots of opportunities.
  • Contribute to the Annual Fund.  So far, only 50% of you have done so.  Please give a contribution of any amount.  Join the team! You'll be glad you did!

SERVICE HOURS

I want to be sure that everyone is clear about what can be counted toward service hours.  The following can be counted toward your service hours.

  • Chaperone field trips
  • Any work done at the school whether in the classroom or outside including teaching a studio, cleaning, and maintenance tasks.
  • Doing work for the teacher or school at home includes recycling and laundry
  • Helping with events like the Yard Sale or Spring Gala
  • Attendance at Community Meetings and Parent Education nights
  • Sharing your skills or knowledge with a class

The following may NOT be counted toward your service hours.

  • Attendance at parent/teacher/student conferences
  • Attendance at cultural festivals, performances, socials, portfolio afternoons
