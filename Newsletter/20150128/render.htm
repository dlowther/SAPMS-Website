COMMUNITY MEETING

We hope to see you all tonight at our Community Meeting.  These meetings are a time for our school community to come together to explore issues and ideas together.  Families have to opportunity to interact and get to know one another better.  It is our hope that your attendance will help you gain a better understanding of our school's mission, strengths, and challenges. 

 

Your Contract of Commitment requires you to attend two of the three meetings each year.  Attendance at these meetings may be counted toward your service hours. The meeting will begin at 6:00 and be held in the modular classroom.  Remember there are two available parking lots on San Marco a block south of Williams.  ABLE has a small lot on the east side and there is a large lot across the street at the former CVS.  Please do not block our neighbors' driveways. 

 
LOWER ELEMENTARY
In lower elementary, the children have been busy studying the continent of Antarctica. They have learned a great deal about the biome, geography, and the animals.

 
PENGUIN DAY

In lieu of a cultural festival, we will hold Penguin Day this Friday to top off our Antarctica studies.  Children should wear black and white.  This will be a small celebration held in individual classes and for students only. This will be the time your child will share the Antarctica poster that they created at home.  Posters should be no larger than 11" x 14".  They may be exclusively pictures or include pictures and information.  Pictures may be from magazines, the Internet, or hand drawn.  Upper Elementary students are asked to be ready to tell a penguin joke, riddle, or poem.  From Antarctica, we will head north (everything is north from Antarctica) to Australia and Oceania.

 
"SPROUTING SEEDS OF SUCCESS"
SILENT AUCTION ACTION NEEDED!

With only 3 months to go, we have a theme, now we need lots of silent auction donations!  This is really where we need everyone to chip in and help! Silent auction donations is where 95% of the money will be raised from the gala for the school.    

 
We have made it easy to help.  First, we are mailing solicitation letters to over 350 local businesses this week.  We will make this list of businesses viewable to everyone.  You can choose businesses from this list you want to be charge of following up with after they receive the letters.  You can also ADD new businesses you want to ask.  The best business to ask are those that you frequent (ex. restaurants, salons, gyms, friends that work at local establishments, etc).  
 
Second, here is a link to the solicitation letter and form. Please print and take this with you as you patron local businesses and ask for their support through an in-kind donation. Feel free to personalize it and email it to businesses as well.  Businesses really are willing to donate items or services.  It is way easier than asking for straight money.  It is just a matter of following up with the businesses and taking the time to pick up items.
