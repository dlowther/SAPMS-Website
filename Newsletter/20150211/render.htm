STUDENT DEMONSTRATION NIGHT


 

We enjoyed a wonderful parent education evening last week. The kids did a great job presenting their lessons, and I think some parents learned a few things. I did notice that a few parents pulled out their phones to check on the answers derived from the math materials and were delighted to see that they were correct.  Here's what some of our parents had to say about their experience.


"The poise, composure, maturity, and presence each child had was a very touching and moving experience."


"It was a really neat and faith strengthening in our school to see the children learning and teaching."


"I loved the confidence the kids had presenting lessons."


"I was impressed with the seriousness with which each child taught the subject materials and how pleased they were to be able to teach others."

 

" The student teachers were not only able to demonstrate the lesson but were able to vary them to suit the level of the person they were giving it to.  Younger students who might someday attend SAPMS were excited and attentive to learn from the big kids." 


"I could not have been more proud of the children and our Montessori method of teaching."

 

We may do this again in the spring.  If you missed it this time be sure to make it to the next one!

 
GRANDPARENT'S DAY

We look forward to welcoming grandparents to our school.  We will gather on the back deck at 10:00 (change from 9:00).  Then grandparents will have the opportunity to join their grandchild in his or her classroom.

 
VALENTINE'S DAY

Remember, students who choose to bring Valentines must bring one for everyone in their class.  There are 18 children in each LE class and 24 in UE.  LE students please do not address envelopes.   Children will decorate a bag to receive their Valentines.  We ask that you do not bring candy or other sweets to school that day.

OPEN ENROLLMENT

We are currently accepting applications for the coming school year.  If you know of any families interested in a free Montessori education for their child, please direct them to our website where they can download an application.  In order to be entered in the lottery, applications must be submitted by February 28.  Enrollment forms for siblings of currently enrolled students must also be submitted by February 28.

"SPROUTING SEEDS OF SUCCESS"
SILENT AUCTION ACTION NEEDED!

With only 2 short months to go, the race is on to get much needed silent auction donations!  This is really where we need everyone to chip in and help! Silent auction donations is where 95% of the money will be raised from the gala for the school.    

 

We have made it super easy to help!.  First, we are mailed solicitation letters to over 350 local businesses several weeks ago.   This real time progress list of businesses solicited and donations received is viewable to everyone by on the Google Spreadsheet.  If you have trouble viewing this google drive spreadsheet, contact Keri Matthews at kcmflorida@yahoo.com.


 

You can choose businesses from this list OR add new businesses you want to ask.  The best business to ask are those that you frequent (ex. restaurants, salons, gyms, friends that work at local establishments, etc). 


Second, here is a link to the solicitation letter and form. Please print and take this with you as you patron local businesses and ask for their support through an in-kind donation. Feel free to personalize it and email it to businesses as well.  Businesses really are willing to donate items or services.  It is way easier than asking for straight money.  It is just a matter of following up with the businesses and taking the time to pick up items.

When you pick up items and certificates for the silent auction, you can either give them directly to Leilanie or drop them off at the school in an envelope marked for Leillanie.  

Also, please join us for our fundraising meeting on Thursday, Feb. 12th at 6pm at Pacific Asian Bistro, located at 159 Palencia Village Dr. Suite 111, St. Aug 32095.  The meeting will be devoted to planning our Silent Auction Gala, Sprouting Seeds of Success.  It is a great way to get involved and support the school!

 

A huge thank you to Lielanie for chairing this year's Gala! Please contact her if you have any questions or can offer your help -  lmlamd@yahoo.com. With everyone's support, it is sure to be a huge success for our children and our school!
