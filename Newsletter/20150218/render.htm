GRANDPARENTS' DAY

Our first Grandparents' Day was a big success. We had a great turnout in each classroom. The children were thrilled to have the opportunity to show their school work to their grandparents. It was hard to tell who was having the most fun, the students or the elders. Thanks to all the grandparents who were able to attend. This will definitely be an annual event on our school calendar.
