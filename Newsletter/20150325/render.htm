CONFERENCES

If you haven't signed up for a conference time, please contact your child's teacher. Remember that students are expected to attend and even run these conferences.  Let the teacher know if you need a few minutes to speak privately, and we will accommodate that. Be sure to arrive on time and be courteous and take no more than the allotted 30 minutes. If you arrive late, your conference time will be shortened.  Thank you for following these guidelines.

 
TESTING

Third and fourth grade students will take the Florida Standards Assessment this week and next. The math assessment will be this Thursday, 3/26 and Friday, 3/27 with reading and language arts assessment next Tuesday, 3/31 and Wednesday, 4/1.  Please ensure your child is well rested and has a nutritious breakfast high in protein and low in sugar.  Get a quiet start to your day with no TV or other screen time. Arrive at school on time.  No other preparation is needed.

 
SPRING GALA

I will see you there!

 
SCHOOL HOLIDAY AND PORTFOLIO AFTERNOON
