BACK BY POPULAR DEMAND

Our final Parent Education evening is just two weeks away on Wednesday, May 13.  Because I have received so many requests, we will repeat our Student Demonstration Night.  This is the evening where our students give lessons to the parents.  Each student is responsible for one lesson, which he or she presents to all interested adults. The ability to do this requires students to utilize higher level thinking skills.  In addition to understanding the material concept, they must synthesize and apply that knowledge to a new context. You will be amazed at the children's self-assurance and ability to answer questions.
 

If you missed this last time, be sure to make it to this one.  You will be glad you did.  For those of you who have not attended a parent education event, you MUST attend this one in order to fulfill the terms of your Contract of Commitment. 

 

Williams Street Clean-up 

After testing last week, a group of UE students walked down Williams Street to the Fountain of Youth and back picking up trash.  As you can see in just those few blocks, we completely filled a trash bag.  

 
COMMUNITY MEETING

Mark your calendars for Tuesday, May 26. This is our last community meeting, and again, if you have only attended one of the two previous meetings, you MUST attend this one.  These meetings are for you; they provide an opportunity to hear about what is going on at our school, ask questions, and provide input.  Additionally, you get to interact with other school parents and staff.  If there is sufficient interest, child care will be provided for a $5 donation to the care givers.

 
NEW PARENT ORIENTATION

All new parents are required to attend a four hour parent orientation class. These are held in the spring and summer.  The next class is on Saturday, June 3 from 10:00 - 3:00 with a one hour lunch break.  If you joined our school community midway through the school year, please make plans to attend this.  We will discuss Montessori philosophy, practice, along with school policies and procedures.
