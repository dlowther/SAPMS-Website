STUDENT DEMONSTRATION NIGHT

 We are repeating this popular parent education event next Wednesday, May 13 at 6:15.  Children will each have a lesson that they will present to the adults present.  This is your chance to get your hands on those marvelous Montessori materials.  Even if you are a Lower Elementary parent, be sure to stop in to the Upper Elementary classroom. Be prepared to be challenged by some of the lessons. Parents of third year students are especially encouraged to come over to see what is ahead for your child next year. Remember, if you have not attended the two previous parent education evenings, your Contract of Commitment requires your attendance at this one. 

 
DISCOVERY EDUCATION TESTING

We have begun this district required testing with our first year students this week. Second year students will be tested next week and third year students the following week. Students are tested in reading and math with each test taking about 30 minutes. 

 
STUDENT VISITS

Starting next week each third year student will have an opportunity to visit in the Upper Elementary classroom.  Students will visit with another third year friend and stay for the morning and lunch.  This will help them feel more prepared for the transition coming next year.
