OUR FAMILIES ARE THE BEST!

Thank you for taking the time to recognize the efforts of your children's teachers last week.  Everyday provided a new treat.  We loved our fruit baskets, enjoyed sandwiches and sweet treats, coffee, flowers, and a veritable feast on Friday.  Probably the best of all were the sweet and heartfelt notes from the children.  Thanks to Angela Thompson for organizing the group effort and to all the families who sent individual tokens of appreciation.  We appreciated being pampered by you.  Most of all, thank you for sharing your beautiful children with us.
