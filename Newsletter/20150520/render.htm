FACILITY UPDATE

You may have noticed the renovation activities across the street.  Our landlord, Authentic Old Jail, Inc./Historic Tours of America, is turning the old Mary Peck house into Upper Elementary classrooms.  We have signed our lease and expect a completion date of July 1.

 
ENROLLMENT UPDATE

We will be adding two new classrooms to SAPMS for the 2015-16 school year, adding 36 additional students for a total of 96.  New parent Montessori classes are being conducted in June and July.  We have a waitlist for most grades but all interested students should submit an application.  We usually experience a lot of movement on the waitlist over the summer. 
FACULTY UPDATE

We are so fortunate and excited about our faculty for 2015-16.  All current lead teachers are returning!  Ms. Kirby, Ms. Heather, and Ms. Gwen will lead our three Lower Elementary classes.  Ms. Lorraine and Ms. Melanie will lead our two Upper Elementary classes.  Ms. Gwen and Ms. Melanie, both experienced teachers, are excited to undertake their Montessori training this summer with Seacoast Center.  Ms. Chelsea, who took over for Ms. Nancy will return next year to continue as Lower Elementary assistant.  Mr. Chris will work full time as our Exceptional Student Education and remediation teacher. 
PARKING REMINDER

Please model patience and courtesy with parking and drop-off and pick-up as we end the school year.  As we add an additional building, more students, more cars, and a crosswalk to the SAPMS facility, we need to be mindful of the residential neighborhood.  Please park along San Marco or in the vacant parking lots across San Marco and walk to the school for the Australia Festival and the Moving Up End of Year Potluck.  There is a water well in the lot west of the school that was damaged during one of our recent school events.  Please do not park in any grassed areas, easements, or yards in the residential neighborhood. 
COMMUNITY MEETING - TUES., MAY 26

Our last Community Meeting of the school year will be held next Tuesday, May 26.  We will meet in the modular classroom at 6:00.  This will be your opportunity to hear more about our new building, teachers, and plans for next year. See the coveted Sea Turtle award presented to our volunteer of the year.  Past recipients were Jean McDowell, Rob & Keri Matthews, and Carole Loughlin.  As always, you will have time to ask questions and interact with other parents of our school community.  Our new teachers will also be on hand to meet you. And if all of that is not enough, you can count your attendance toward service hours. 

 

Child care for school age children will be provided if there is sufficient interest. There will be a $5 donation to the caregiver. Please email me if you will use this service.  Remember, we do not have a safe environment to provide toddler care.  

 

STUDENT DEMONSTRATION NIGHT

This was a roaring success.  Students loved being the teachers and did a fabulous job.  Parents got a chance to get their hands on those marvelous Montessori materials and did a fabulous job as well.

 
UPCOMING SPECIAL EVENTS

The Upper Elementary class will enjoy a trip to Whitney Lab tomorrow, Thursday, May 21. They will handle live marine animals and conduct experiments and investigations.  On the following Thursday, May 28 the entire school will head over to the Alligator Farm. There is a $9.50 charge for this.


 Our Australia Festival will be held on Tuesday, June 2 at 1:00.  We have arranged for a presentation by Didgeridoo Down Under.  This interactive performance will feature Australian artifacts, Aboriginal art, and of course, didgeridoos. Since there is a charge for the performance, we ask that each family contribute $9 per student.  Families are invited to attend.  After the performance we will have Australian/Oceania food tasting.

 

Wednesday, June 3 is our Moving Up Ceremony and potluck dinner.  All students are recognized for their successful year at the Montessori school.  This is followed by a potluck dinner.


Friday, June 4 is the last day of school.  All students will be dismissed at noon.  There will be no aftercare available.

 

A big thanks to Lisa and Jamie Rowell, for sharing their rescue fawn and baby gray foxes with Ms. Heather's class, for our conclusion with our studies of the five classes of vertebrates.
ANNUAL FUND CONTRIBUTORS

We would like to take this opportunity to recognize and thank the families, staff, board, and friends who contributed to our Annual Fund this year.  Your support is vital to the success of our school. Know that you make a difference for our children.  Thank you!
