IT'S SUMMERTIME (ALMOST)

Our third year at St. Augustine Public Montessori School is rapidly drawing to a close. What a great year! It has been a privilege and honor to educate your children this year.  Together we have learned much and shared experiences that will be remembered for years to come.  We will soon return your children to you, a little taller and a few pounds heavier, children who have learned to function independently and take responsibility for their own learning.  We encourage you to continue to foster the development of this independence over the summer.  We all celebrate the many wonderful learning experiences and educational opportunities offered to our joyful scholars in the Montessori learning environment.

 

Thanks to all of you who have contributed in ways large and small this year and for supporting our work in countless ways.  We notice and appreciate.  Special thanks and congratulations go out to Teri Aboulafia, who was presented with the Sea Turtle award at Tuesday's Community Meeting.  This award is given each year to the volunteer who epitomizes the spirit of service at our school.  It is not an easy task to choose just one recipient, but Teri has been there working and advocating for the school since the beginning.

 

To those of you leaving us for new places and new adventures, we wish you all the best.  Congratulations to our two very capable sixth graders who will be moving on to the next phase of their educations.   We will miss you.

 

May your summer be filled with play, exploration, learning, and plenty of time spent outdoors.

 
LAST OF THE YEAR

This is the last newsletter for this school year.  Look for an update over the summer. Many thanks to Jacquelyn Zeichner who produces this letter each week.  She takes my ramblings plus submissions from teachers and parents and turns it into a great looking newsletter ready for your enjoyment.

 

This is also the last week for Bricks 4 Kidz,after school yoga, and pizza. 

 
MOVING UP CEREMONY

Join us at 6:00 on Wednesday, June 3rd for our Moving Up Ceremony.  We will gather to honor our children for their year of hard work and learning.  Our third year and sixth year students will receive special recognition. Before the ceremony the children will share a song. 

 

The ceremony will be followed by a pot luck dinner.  Bring a dish or drink to share along with your own reusable plates, utensils, cups, and napkins for each family member.  Come with your chair, camera, friends and family.

 

If you still need service hours for this year, I will give double hours for help cleaning up after this event. Email me if interested. 

 
LAST DAY OF SCHOOL

The last day of school is Thursday, June 4th.  All students will be dismissed at noon.  There will be no lunch and no aftercare available. Please plan accordingly for noon pick up.  Thank you.

 
UPCOMING SPECIAL EVENTS

Our Australia Festival will be held on Tuesday, June 2 at 1:00.  We have arranged for a presentation by Didgeridoo Down Under.  This interactive performance will feature Australian artifacts, Aboriginal art, and of course, didgeridoos. Since there is a charge for the performance, we ask that each family contribute $9 per student.  Families are invited to attend.  After the performance we will have Australian/Oceania food tasting.
