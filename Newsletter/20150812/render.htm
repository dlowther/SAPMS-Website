Welcome to the New School Year!

On behalf of the staff and board of St Augustine Montessori School, I am happy to welcome you to the 2015-16 school year.  We are looking forward to a productive partnership with you to ensure our children achieve their highest potential.  We know that in order for children to be successful, they will need support from both the school and home.  As partners, we share responsibility for our children's success. 

Here at the school, we provide a learning environment where children are actively engaged in their learning.  The adults in the environment facilitate the child's natural process of learning while taking into consideration the needs of the whole child.  Limits are set in a fair, firm, and consistent manner and children are free to make choices within those limits.  They are also responsible for accepting the consequences of their choices.  This leads to the development of self-discipline.

We ask that you guide and support your child's learning by ensuring that he/she:
  • Attends school daily and arrives on time.
  • Starts the day with a nutritious breakfast high in protein
  • Has a healthy lunch that can be accessed independently
  • Reads (or is read to) daily to develop a love for reading and to improve literacy skills.
  • Gets a good night's sleep.
We ask that you:
  • Read all communication from school.
  • Attend Community Meetings and Parent Education events
  • Attend parent/student/teacher conferences.
  • Read the Community Handbook which you will receive next week
  • Become an active participant in the life of the school.
Together we can improve the quality of the school community and provide the best for our children. We look forward to your support and the opportunity to get to know you.

Drop Off and Pick Up
We have many new families this year. Please bear with us this week, as we get to know all the families and cars.  It will help if you place your child's name tag in your windshield at pick up.  Please DO NOT ENTER THE CAR LINE MORE THAN FIVE MINUTES BEFORE PICK UP.  When cars start lining up at 2:30, traffic gets backed down the street.   Plan to arrive closer to 3:00. Also, do not line up on the left side of the street until you enter our driveway. If you need to wait to enter the car line, use the right side of the street. This is very important! We have green trains, red trains, and many delivery trucks using Williams St. You may also park around the corner on San Marco and walk to the gate for pick up or drop off.  We will make adjustments as needed, as we see how this week goes. 

If you are interested in car pooling, sign the list in the Williams St. office. I'll also send the list to the Galimore Center.  You may then contact others on the list coming from your area.
Community Meeting
Our first Community Meeting of the year is next Tuesday, August 18 at 6:00.  Due to our increased enrollment, we will meet at the Doubletree Inn on San Marco Ave.  The Doubletree has graciously made their ballroom available to us at no charge.  Childcare will be available here at the school for a donation of $5/child.  We can only offer childcare for school age children.  We do not have a safe environment for children under five.  RSVP to sandi@staugustinemontessori.com if you intend to use this service.

At this meeting you will meet our staff and board and hear about our vision, mission, and finances.  This is always a great opportunity to get to know the other families at the school.  Your Contract of Commitment requires you to attend two of the three Community Meetings.  The meeting time also counts towards your 20 service hours.

I look forward to seeing everyone there.
