First Community Meeting

We had a great turnout for last night's Community Meeting with 55 families represented.  Remember, your Contract of Commitment requires you to attend two of the three Community Meetings held each year.  So if you did not attend last night, you are required to attend the next two.

We started the evening with a classification exercise where parents placed their names under any heading that applied to them. This let everyone see with whom they have something in common.

Staff and board members were introduced. Jean McDowell, President of the Board of Trustees announced that we are seeking new parent board members.  If interested, you can find the application process on our website, www.staugustinemontessori.com.  Board priorities for this year are: update Strategic Plan, charter renewal, long term facility search, development of a fundraising plan, identification of community outreach opportunities, consideration of increased employment benefits, and support for Director, faculty, and staff. Director priorities include, staff development, facility improvement and beautification, and parent education and involvement.

Parent volunteer tasks include fundraising leads, science fair volunteers, art show lead, parent library coordinator, and lunch and recess volunteers.  Our major fundraising event is the Gala, which will be held in February this school year.  We will have our first meeting for this on Wednesday, September 2 at 6:00 in the modular classroom. To pull off a successful event, we need many hands to share the work. I hope to see you at the meeting.

Parent, Teri Aboulafia explained the different passive fundraisers in which we participate.  These include Target Red Card, Amazon Smile, I Give, Shoparoo, and Box Tops for Education.  Teri prepared a handout explaining these.  If you weren't at the meeting, ask us to get one to you.
Everyone should have received an electronic copy of the 15-16 Community Handbook.  This is your navigation guide for the school year.  Please take the time to read it.  Hard copies were distributed at the meeting.  If you did not attend, one will be sent home in your child's folder.  You may want to print the School Calendar, which is the last page in the electronic copy.
Lower Elementary Drop Off
Families of Lower Elementary students have a 15 minute window in the morning for drop off, 8:00 - 8:15.  Last year the gate stayed open until 8:30 for Upper Elementary drop off.  Many LE parents used that extra 15 minutes.  This year the gate will close at 8:15 so our staff can get to their classrooms.  If you arrive after 8:15, you will have to park, walk your child into the building, and sign the late arrival log.  Sandi or I will escort your child to the classroom.  Thank you for your attention to this.
Pizza Fridays
Our first pizza day will be Friday, September 4.  You will receive an order form to order for the entire first semester.  This order form will take into account any days we are not in school for lunch on Friday. You may also pay by the week.  Weekly payments need to be into the office by Thursday.  Place payment in an envelope or plastic bag labeled with your child's name. The cost for pizza is $5/week and includes a side of fruit.  You will still want to send in a snack for your children and make sure they have their water bottles.
