International Day of Peace

We were quite an impressive group on Monday.  How beautiful that the great turnout was in support of peace!  In her later years, Dr. Montessori devoted herself to peace education.  She saw child-centered education as a way to radically transform society to protect humankind from the threat of destructive warfare.  In 1932 she challenged the common perception of peace as the cessation of war.  She saw peace as a world where justice and love create harmony among all peoples.

She described the transformation required for this new stage of society as a new stage in evolution. She saw education as the means to bring this step forward. Her idea was that  Cosmic Education would lead children to grow up to create a new kind of society, where peace is based on the understanding of the fundamental unity of humanity. Maria Montessori was nominated for the Nobel Peace prize three times for her unique approach to peace.
