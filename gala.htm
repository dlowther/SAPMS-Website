SILENT AUCTION SNEAK PEAK!

Pre-Gala On-Line Bidding is Now Open!

 We are so excited about some of our auction items, that we can no longer keep them a secret! Our "Online Auction" for early bidding is now open for select vacation packages, artwork and celebrity memorabilia!

The highest online bids will become the minimum bids for the Live and Silent Auctions during the Gala. If the item is not outbid during the Gala, the Online Bidder wins. This is a great way to get ahead of the game if there is an item you really have your eye on - or for friends and family who will not be able to attend the Gala on April 21st. Feel free to forward the link below - as anyone can participate and need not be present at the Gala to win.

Participating is easy:

Go to www.32auctions.com/SAPMSAuction2013
Create a new account (it's free!) and request access
An auction administrator will grant access and you may then browse the items and begin bidding.

Good Luck!
