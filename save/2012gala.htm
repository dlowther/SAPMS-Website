A Celebration of Education:
The 1st Annual Fundraising Gala and Auction, benefitting the students of the
St Augustine Public Montessori School.

Thursday, May 31st, 2012
6pm - 9pm
The White Room in Historic Downtown St Augustine
Live Music by Baroka
DJ Andy Calvert of High Fidelity
Hors D'Oeuvres and Light Buffet
Live Auction hosted by Great Expectations Auctioneer
Silent Auction
Cash Bar with Tickets

Join us for A Celebration of Education at The White Room - located in the heart of downtown. Experience live music and entertainment; participate in exciting live and silent auctions; enjoy delicious hors d’oeuvres; and - from the private bayfront balcony in the elegant Main Ballroom - enjoy the best water views Historic St. Augustine has to offer.
A very memorable evening for a very worthwhile cause.

Tickets are available at $75 per person, which include hors d’oeuvres, light buffet stations and two drink tickets. Click here to purchase tickets now.
